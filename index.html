Strategy

A simple core — with active edges

We maintain a concentrated core in Bitcoin‑linked assets ("Digital Capital") and selectively allocate to AI infrastructure, compute, and energy ("Digital Intelligence"). Edge comes from dynamic HODL, disciplined rebalancing, and opportunistic pair trades.

Digital Capital (BTC)

Core exposure to Bitcoin and Bitcoin‑treasury equities; focus on unitized accounting, liquidity, and tax efficiency.

Digital Intelligence (AI)

Select AI infrastructure: compute, datacenters, energy. Seek asymmetric payoffs with strong downside framing.

Dynamic HODL + Risk

Rebalancing bands, pair trades, options overlays, and cash management to smooth unit volatility and protect compounding.
